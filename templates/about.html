About Us

❝ Photography is a way of feeling, touching & loving. What you have caught on film is captured forever... It remembers little things, long after you have forgotten everything. In photography there is a reality so subtle that it becomes more real than the reality. We here at Vintage Studios are making photographs to understand what our lives mean to us, thus cherishing your precious moments. Here we're a team of observers having a keen eye capturing the magical moments happening around us. We started our visual journey officially in 2016, in the city of Pune, India serving various individuals and businesses to build their brands. We're professionals having a variety of exposure in the field of Visuals, having good hands on photography & cinematic videos. The whole point of taking pictures is so that you don't have to explain things with words, so we're happy to help you with that anytime you need us! Professional photography is all about using out of the box skills & high quality equipment to produce exceptional quality, unique and visually stunning imagery. ❞

Why professional photography

  • Using professional photography immediately raises the perception of quality associated with your brand.
  • Professional photography is incredibly versatile, and can be used across all sorts of media; whether on your website, in print ads, email banners or social media posts.
  • A skilled photographer helps you get the best out of your images using the perfect lighting, and capturing the subject at the best angle, but a photographer can also help you capture the essence of your brand, and establish your brand identity – bringing out what’s unique about your business.
  • Photography is aspirational as it helps potential customers visualise your product or service as part of their lives, and gives them an idea of how investing in your business will benefit them.
  • Professional photography is a must for any business helping your potential customers identify you from the universe of other businesses similar to you, increasing traffic to your website, selling the benefits of your product or service, increasing the perception of quality, and is often of such a high resolution that it can be used cross-platform, forming the backbone of a strong campaign and brand identity.
