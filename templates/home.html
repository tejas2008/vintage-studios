FAQ

What type of visual work do you specialise in ?

We specialise in product & interior photography, also with great cinematic videography skills.

Which areas do you provide photography services?

We provide services Pan India & Globally as well. Our base is in Pune, India. Travel charges applicable as per location, local shoots are travel charge free.

When does the service needs to be booked?

You need to book a prior date for your selected service at least a week before. Urgent booking slots are subject to availability and might be charged extra.*

What are the payment terms?

Advance payment 25% of the total service charges if the amount is above ₹20,000 or 50% of the total service charges if the total amount is below ₹20,000 to secure the date and time. Or you may choose to settle it all at booking time itself. Remaining payment to be completed after the shoot, prior to photo/video delivery. Repeat clients may be able to arrange a credit account* depends upon the service requirement.
